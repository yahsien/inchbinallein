Wisdom Consulting Advises Champion Microelectronic on a US$75mn Public Listing

  • March 21, 2011
    Contact

  • Wisdom Consulting, a leading boutique consulting firm to start up and established information technology and health sciences companies, announced today it has advised Champion Microelectronic on a US$75 million public listing. The proceeds will be used to fund Champion Microelectronic's capital investment needs.

    The Wisdom team was led by chief executive officer David Lai, and included vice president Eric Shen, and associates First Securities.

    “It’s an honor to work once again with Champion Microelectronic, a company with which we maintain a close relationship,” said David Lai. "We are proud to have helped our client achieve its goals and to close such a complex transaction in such a short period”.

The firm is staffed by a team of experienced financial industry professionals and is recognized among its peers for its commitment to transaction execution.
