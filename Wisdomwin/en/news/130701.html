  • Wisdom Consulting, a leading boutique consulting firm to start up and established information technology and health sciences companies, announced today that it has appointed Mr. Ross Feingold as a senior counsel.

    “I am pleased to announce the appointment of Mr. Ross Feingold as senior counsel and a member of our team,” said David Lai, chief executive officer of Wisdom Consulting. “We are very proud to have his join our firm and contribute his expertise.” Feingold advises clients in transactions related to information technology.

    He received his B.S. from Bucknell University, and his juris doctor from American University. Earlier in her career, Feingold worked at The Royal Bank of Scotland.

    • *** About Wisdom Consulting ***

  • Wisdom Consulting is a Taipei, Taiwan based consulting firm that specializes in Finance Placement, Business Advisory and Business Development services. The firm is staffed by a team of experienced financial industry professionals and is recognized among its peers for its commitment to transaction execution.
