Wisdom Consulting Advises Revlis Biotech on a US$15mn Private Placement

  • June 28, 2013
    Contact
    Vanessa Liu
    +886 2 2 2501 5533
    press@wisdomwin.com.tw

  • Wisdom Consulting, a leading boutique consulting firm to start up and established information technology and health sciences companies, announced today it has advised Revlis Biotech on a US$15 million private placement. The proceeds will be used to fund Revlis Biotech's capital investment needs.

    The Wisdom team was led by chief executive officer David Lai, and included vice president Eric Shen, and associates Yuanta Financial Group. The transaction was notable for being the first time a private operated biotechnology firm in Taiwan raised this level of financing.

    “It’s an honor to work once again with Revlis Biotech, a company with which we maintain a close relationship,” said David Lai. "We are proud to have helped our client achieve its goals and to close such a complex transaction in such a short period”.
