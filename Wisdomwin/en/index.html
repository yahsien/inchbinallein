Wisdom/智創管理顧問有限公司

Wisdom Consulting is a boutique advisory firm that advises clients from, or doing business in, China, Hong Kong and Taiwan, with a focus on information technology and health sciences.

Wisdom's services include finance placement, business advisory, and business development. Wisdom's principals have over 20 years of experience in Greater China financial services, and have worked in both local and global financial institutions, such as Goldman Sachs, Yuanta Financial Group, and Intel Capital. Their experience spans from sell side research covering some of region's most prominent companies in the information technology products supply chain, to fund management covering multiple strategies and asset classes.

Wisdom finance placement capability has allowed it to assist its clients to raise over US$100 million via private and public placements since 2011. Our consulting team advised a medical devices company in its successful application for a European CE certification. Using our information technology industry knowledge we helped a client build a cloud-based operating platform and BIG DATA analysis software, so that the client may pursue further growth in markets throughout the world.

Wisdom's multinational team of experts allows it to combine both local and global experience to successfully execute transactions, and Wisdom's client companies are based both in Asia and the United States.
