Eric Shen, Vice President, Business Development

Eric Shen has 15 years of experience in Taiwan's financial industry. He is experienced in investment analysis, including company research, as well as corporate finance and capital raising advisory work.

Earlier in his career, Mr. Shen was a securities analyst at local securities house Core Pacific, as well as global investment bank Nomura. Mr. Shen published research on sectors including financial services, integrated circuit design, optoelectronics, and system integrators.

Mr. Shen is a Taiwan national and holds a Taiwan investment analyst license. In addition to his native Mandarin and Taiwanese, Mr. Shen is also fluent in English. Mr. Shen holds a Master of Science in International Securities, Investment and Banking from The University of Reading （ICMA Center）and a Master of Science in International Business from South Bank University, both in England, and a Bachelor of Science in Statistics from Tunghai University in Taiwan.
