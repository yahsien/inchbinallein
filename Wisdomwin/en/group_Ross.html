Ross Feingold, Counsel

Ross Feingold has extensive experience in cross border transactions in Asia from his previous investment bank and law firm experience.

He previously worked in the Hong Kong office The Royal Bank of Scotland and Deutsche Bank, and the Singapore office of J.P. Morgan. He also worked in the Taipei office of international law firm Russin & Vecchi. Earlier in his career, Mr. Feingold worked in the equity research department at Jardine Fleming.

Mr. Feingold received his undergraduate degree from Bucknell University and juris doctor from American University. He is admitted to practice law in New York and Washington DC. Mr. Feingold studied Mandarin at National University of Singapore and National Taiwan Normal University.
