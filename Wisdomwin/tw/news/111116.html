Wisdom Consulting Appointed by Medgaea Life Sciences Ltd.

  • November 16, 2011

  • Wisdom Consulting, a leading boutique consulting firm to start up and established information technology and health sciences companies, announced today that it was appointed by Medgaea Life Sciences to act as a business development and corporate finance consultant.

    David Lai, who heads Wisdom's business development consulting team, will head the project, and will be assisted by Eric Shen. Both Lai and Shen are experts in assisting biotechnology companies to expand their business in Greater China.

    Lai said, “We are honored to add Medgaea Life Sciences to our roster of clients, and look forward to working with them to open additional markets for their innovative services to market index research”.

    • *** About Wisdom Consulting ***

  • Wisdom Consulting is a Taipei, Taiwan based consulting firm that specializes in Finance Placement, Business Advisory and Business Development services. The firm is staffed by a team of experienced financial industry professionals and is recognized among its peers for its commitment to transaction execution.
